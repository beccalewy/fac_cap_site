Co-founder and CEO of HuggingFace

Clement Delangue is a visionary entrepreneur in the field of artificial intelligence and natural language processing. As the co-founder and CEO of HuggingFace, he has been instrumental in democratizing AI and making cutting-edge NLP technologies accessible to developers and researchers worldwide.

Under Clement's leadership, HuggingFace has become a hub for the AI community, offering open-source libraries, pre-trained models, and collaborative tools that have significantly accelerated the progress of NLP research and applications.

Prior to HuggingFace, Clement co-founded Moodstocks, a mobile image recognition company that was acquired by Google in 2016. His expertise in AI and entrepreneurship has made him a respected figure in the tech industry.
